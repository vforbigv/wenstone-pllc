ANTI-SPAM POLICY

Our Commitment

At Wenstone Law PLLC, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of communication with our clients and website visitors. This Anti-Spam Policy outlines our approach to electronic communications and our commitment to preventing unsolicited commercial messages.

What is Spam?

Spam refers to unsolicited, bulk electronic messages, typically of a commercial nature, sent without the recipient's consent or with misleading subject lines or content.

Our Practices

Wenstone Law PLLC adheres to the following practices:

  • We only send electronic communications to individuals who have explicitly requested to receive such communications or with whom we have an existing business relationship.
  • We include clear and accurate information about the sender of the message.
  • We provide a simple and effective way for recipients to opt out of receiving future communications.
  • We promptly honor all opt-out requests.

Email Collection and Use

We collect email addresses through legitimate means, such as through our website contact form or direct client interactions. We use these email addresses solely for the purpose of providing information about our services, responding to inquiries, or maintaining client relationships.

Opt-Out Procedure

All our electronic communications include a clear method to opt out of future communications. If you wish to stop receiving emails from us, you can:

  • Click the "unsubscribe" link included in our emails
  • Contact us directly at contact@wenstonelaw.com with "UNSUBSCRIBE" in the subject line

Compliance with Laws

Wenstone Law PLLC complies with all applicable laws regarding electronic communications, including the CAN-SPAM Act in the United States.

Reporting Spam

If you believe you have received an unsolicited message from us or someone claiming to represent Wenstone Law PLLC, please forward the message to contact@wenstonelaw.com so we can investigate and take appropriate action.

Policy Updates

We may update this Anti-Spam Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
